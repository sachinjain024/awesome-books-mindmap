## About This Mind Map

This mind map captures the essential ideas from Chris Guillebeau's "The $100 Startup," a guide to building a micro-business that provides both income and freedom. Drawing from case studies of 1,500 individuals who built businesses earning $50,000 or more from a modest investment (often $100 or less), Guillebeau distills the patterns and principles behind successful bootstrapped ventures.

Whether you are a budding entrepreneur, a side-hustler, or someone dreaming of escaping the traditional 9-to-5, this mind map walks you through the complete journey: from discovering the convergence of passion and value, to crafting irresistible offers, launching with impact, and scaling strategically. Each chapter provides actionable frameworks you can apply immediately to start or grow your own micro-business.
