## About This Book

Extreme Ownership is a leadership book forged in the crucible of combat. Written by two former U.S. Navy SEAL officers, Jocko Willink and Leif Babin, who served together in SEAL Team Three's Task Unit Bruiser during the Battle of Ramadi in 2006, one of the most violent and dangerous campaigns of the Iraq War. The lessons they learned under fire translate directly to leadership challenges in business and life.

The book is structured around a simple but powerful framework: each chapter presents a battlefield story from Ramadi, extracts the core leadership principle, then shows how that principle applies to business situations the authors encountered through their leadership consulting firm, Echelon Front. This pattern of combat story, principle, and business application makes the lessons vivid, memorable, and immediately actionable.

Whether you lead a military unit, a corporate team, a startup, or a family, the principles in Extreme Ownership will challenge you to take complete responsibility for your outcomes, simplify your approach, empower your people, and build the discipline that creates true freedom. This mind map distills the twelve core principles into an accessible format that you can study, internalize, and apply in your own leadership journey.
