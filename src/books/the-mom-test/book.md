## About This Mind Map

This mind map distills Rob Fitzpatrick's essential guide to customer conversations into an accessible, structured format. The Mom Test is a short, practical book for anyone building a product or starting a business who needs to talk to customers without being misled by polite lies and hollow encouragement.

The core idea is deceptively simple: stop talking about your idea and start asking about their life. Most founders sabotage their own customer research by fishing for compliments instead of searching for truth. Fitzpatrick provides a clear set of rules and techniques for having conversations that actually reveal whether your business idea is worth pursuing.

This mind map walks through all eight chapters, covering the fundamental rules of good customer conversations, how to avoid the most common traps, and how to build a repeatable process for learning from customers as a team. Whether you are a first-time founder or an experienced product manager, these principles will change how you approach customer discovery.
