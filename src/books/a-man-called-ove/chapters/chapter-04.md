## Being Useful Again

Ove keeps helping: driving lessons, repairs, ladders, practical fixes. The neighborhood slowly starts treating him as indispensable.

Flashbacks reveal Ove's early losses and his meeting with Sonja, who saw beyond his bluntness and gave his life warmth, meaning, and direction.

<div class="insight-box">
<h4>Key Insight</h4>
<p>Usefulness is Ove's language of care. The neighborhood gives him back the role that grief and retirement took away.</p>
</div>

## Key Takeaways

- Purpose often comes through service.
- Love can reshape identity over decades.
- Community can restore what grief strips away.
