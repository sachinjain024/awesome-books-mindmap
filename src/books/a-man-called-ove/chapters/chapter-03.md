## Old Conflict, New Duty

Rune was once Ove's friend, then rival. Now Rune is seriously ill and his wife Anita needs support navigating systems and care decisions. Ove gets involved despite himself.

At the same time, a stray cat appears and quietly becomes part of Ove's routine.

<div class="quote-box">
"We always think there's enough time to do things with other people. Then something happens and we've run out of time."
<div class="attribution">- Fredrik Backman, A Man Called Ove</div>
</div>

<div class="insight-box">
<h4>Key Insight</h4>
<p>Meaning often returns through small commitments: helping a neighbor, feeding a cat, doing one useful thing today.</p>
</div>

## Key Takeaways

- Old resentments can soften in crisis.
- Action matters more than emotional certainty.
- Small responsibilities can stabilize a life.
