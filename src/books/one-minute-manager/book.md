    description: Catch people doing something right, praise them immediately and specifically, tell them how their good work makes you feel, and pause so they can feel the impact.

## About This Mind Map

This mind map presents the core teachings of *The One Minute Manager*, one of the most widely read management books ever published. Written as a parable, the book follows a young man searching for an effective manager and discovering three remarkably simple secrets that transform how leaders manage people and produce results.

Ken Blanchard and Spencer Johnson distilled decades of behavioral science research into a story that can be read in about an hour but applied for a lifetime. The three secrets — One Minute Goals, One Minute Praisings, and One Minute Redirects — form a complete management system built on clarity, positive reinforcement, and honest feedback delivered with respect.

Whether you are a new manager learning the fundamentals, a seasoned leader looking to simplify your approach, or someone who wants to understand how the best managers bring out the best in people, this mind map captures each secret in detail with practical steps you can begin using immediately.
