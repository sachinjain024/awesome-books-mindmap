## About This Book

Multipliers is the result of extensive research by Liz Wiseman into why some leaders double the output and capability of their teams while others cut it in half. Drawing on interviews with over 150 leaders across four continents, Wiseman identifies two dramatically different leadership styles and the specific behaviors that distinguish them.

The book is essential reading for anyone in a leadership role, whether you manage a small team or run a large organization. It challenges the assumption that the smartest leader in the room should do all the thinking, and instead reveals how the best leaders create genius in others. The five disciplines provide a concrete framework for shifting from a command-and-control style to one that amplifies collective intelligence.

This mind map distills Wiseman's research into an accessible format, covering each of the five disciplines with their contrasting behaviors, practical exercises, and the journey of becoming a Multiplier. Whether you suspect you might be an accidental diminisher or want to sharpen your multiplying skills, these chapters offer actionable insights for making everyone around you smarter and more capable.
