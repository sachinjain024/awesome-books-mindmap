## About This Mind Map

This mind map presents Simon Sinek's influential framework for understanding how great leaders and organizations inspire action. At its core, the book introduces the Golden Circle — a simple but powerful model that explains why some leaders and companies are able to inspire while others are not. Sinek draws on examples from Apple, Martin Luther King Jr., the Wright Brothers, and Southwest Airlines to show that people do not buy what you do, they buy why you do it.

Whether you are a leader, entrepreneur, or anyone seeking to inspire others, this mind map breaks down each chapter into accessible sections with key insights, real-world examples, and practical takeaways. The book challenges the conventional approach of starting with what you do, and makes a compelling case for starting with why you do it — because that is what truly moves people to action.
