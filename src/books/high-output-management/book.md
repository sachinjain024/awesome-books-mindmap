## About This Book

This mind map presents Andy Groves legendary management handbook written from his experience building Intel into a global powerhouse. Grove applies his engineering mindset to management treating it as a production process with measurable outputs and leverage points. This book is a favorite among Silicon Valley leaders for its practical no-nonsense approach to getting results through people.

The book is organized around a core insight a managers output is not what they personally produce but the output of their organization and the neighboring organizations they influence. This reframes everything from how you spend time to how you measure success. Grove walks through production principles leverage meetings decision-making organizational structure and people management all with concrete examples and frameworks you can apply immediately.

What makes this mind map valuable is how it preserves Groves engineering precision while making the concepts easy to navigate. Whether youre a new manager or a seasoned executive youll find frameworks here that clarify what effective management actually means.
