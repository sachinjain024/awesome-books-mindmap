## About This Book

**7 Chapters | 30+ Skills | Millions of Families Helped**

This groundbreaking book offers practical, respectful approaches to communicating with children. Based on the work of child psychologist Dr. Haim Ginott, Faber and Mazlish present specific skills that help parents connect with their children, solve problems, and build lasting relationships based on mutual respect.
