## About This Mind Map

This mind map captures the bold, unconventional business wisdom from Jason Fried and David Heinemeier Hansson, the founders of Basecamp (now 37signals). Rework challenges nearly everything you've been taught about business — from the cult of growth to the worship of workaholism, from the necessity of meetings to the importance of formal planning.

Written in short, punchy chapters that read like manifestos, Rework argues that less is more, constraints are advantages, and the best plan is to start working. Whether you're starting a business or running one, this mind map distills the book's counterintuitive advice into an accessible format that you can return to again and again.
