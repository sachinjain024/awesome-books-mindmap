The Alchemist

A Fable About Following Your Dream by Paulo Coelho

About This Mind Map

This mind map captures the journey of Santiago, a shepherd who follows his Personal Legend from Andalusia to the Egyptian pyramids. Paulo Coelho's beloved fable explores destiny, omens, fear, and the courage to pursue what makes your heart sing.

THE ALCHEMIST
When you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it.

Core Teachings of The Alchemist

  • Personal Legend — Your true purpose in life; when you pursue it, the universe supports you.
  • Omens — The language of the universe; learn to read signs and follow them.
  • Fear of failure — Often worse than failure itself; it keeps you from even trying.
  • The present moment — Life is the moment we're living now; don't live in fear of loss.
  • Listen to your heart — It knows your dreams and speaks the language of the Soul of the World.
