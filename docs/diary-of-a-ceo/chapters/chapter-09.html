Identifying Your First Foundation

Ask yourself these questions:

  • What single activity or condition, when consistently maintained, seems to make everything else better?
  • What single thing, when neglected, creates a disproportionate deterioration across multiple areas of life?

If you find yourself saying "I'll get back to this when things calm down" about a foundational habit, take this as a red flag rather than a reasonable plan.
