What is Booksmap?

Booksmap transforms complex books into accessible, interactive mind maps. Each book is broken down into its key chapters and concepts, presented in a visual format that makes the wisdom easy to understand, navigate, and apply.

Features: Interactive chapter navigation, text highlighting (saved locally), mobile-friendly design, and no account required.

Why Mind Maps for Books?

  • Structured Learning: See the big picture while exploring the details
  • Quick Reference: Find key concepts without re-reading entire chapters
  • Interactive Highlighting: Mark important passages and they persist between visits
  • Free & Open: No accounts, no subscriptions, no tracking
  • Works Offline: Download and use anywhere
