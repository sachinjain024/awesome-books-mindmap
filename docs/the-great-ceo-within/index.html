About This Mind Map

This mind map distills Matt Mochary's practical playbook for startup CEOs and leaders. The book offers tactical, repeatable systems for one-on-ones, feedback, OKRs, meetings, hiring, firing, and communication—drawn from Mochary's experience coaching hundreds of Silicon Valley founders. Whether you're a first-time CEO or scaling a team, these chapters give you the operating system to run your company effectively.

Core Teachings of The Great CEO Within

  • 1:1s are non-negotiable — Regular, subordinate-led one-on-ones are the primary lever for alignment, feedback, and growth.
  • Feedback must be specific and timely — General praise or criticism doesn't change behavior; concrete examples do.
  • OKRs align the organization — Clear objectives and measurable key results keep everyone rowing in the same direction.
  • Write to scale — Written communication scales; spoken communication doesn't. Use memos and updates to inform at scale.
  • Hire slow, fire fast — Raise the bar for hiring and act decisively when someone isn't working out.
  • Culture is what you tolerate — Your values are defined by what you reward and what you let slide.
  • You are the bottleneck — The CEO's job is to remove themselves as the bottleneck by building systems and delegating.
