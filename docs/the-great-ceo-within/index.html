About This Book

This mind map distills Matt Mochary’s practical playbook for startup CEOs and leaders. The book offers tactical, repeatable systems for one-on-ones, feedback, OKRs, meetings, hiring, firing, and communication—drawn from Mochary’s experience coaching hundreds of Silicon Valley founders.

Whether you’re a first-time CEO or scaling a team, these chapters give you the operating system to run your company effectively. From the foundation of 1:1s and feedback to execution through OKRs and meetings, from people systems for hiring and culture to strategy and governance, Mochary provides concrete playbooks you can implement immediately.

The book’s genius is its practicality: every chapter offers specific templates, scripts, and step-by-step processes you can adopt tomorrow.

Core Stoic Principles

  • 1:1s are non-negotiable: Regular, subordinate-led one-on-ones are the primary lever for alignment, feedback, and growth.
  • Feedback must be specific and timely: General praise or criticism doesn't change behavior; concrete examples do.
  • OKRs align the organization: Clear objectives and measurable key results keep everyone rowing in the same direction.
  • Write to scale: Written communication scales; spoken communication doesn't. Use memos and updates to inform at scale.
  • Hire slow, fire fast: Raise the bar for hiring and act decisively when someone isn't working out.
  • Culture is what you tolerate: Your values are defined by what you reward and what you let slide.
  • You are the bottleneck: The CEO's job is to remove themselves as the bottleneck by building systems and delegating.
