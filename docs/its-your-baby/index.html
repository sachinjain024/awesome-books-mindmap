About This Book

It’s Your Baby is a practical parenting guide designed for families navigating pregnancy, newborn care, and the first two years of life. Written in a direct, reassuring style, the book focuses on what new parents actually need in daily life: feeding decisions, sleep routines, common illnesses, vaccines, hygiene, and knowing when medical attention is necessary.

This mind map organizes the book into eight actionable chapters so parents can quickly find guidance during real situations, not just read theory. The goal is to reduce fear, build judgment, and help families raise healthier children with confidence.

Parenting is not about panic. It is about informed, loving decisions made one day at a time. — Dr. Saroja Balan

Core Stoic Principles

  • Observe Before You Panic: Most day-to-day baby issues are manageable when parents first assess behavior, feeding, urine output, sleep, and fever trends.
  • Feeding and Sleep Are Foundational: Regular feeding and predictable sleep rhythms drive growth, immunity, and emotional regulation in early life.
  • Prevention Beats Cure: Vaccines, hygiene, and routine checkups prevent major complications more effectively than reactive treatment.
  • Not Every Internet Tip Applies: Parents should use trusted pediatric guidance, not random online advice, for decisions about medicine and child health.
  • Parents Need Regulation Too: Calm parental response improves infant settling and helps families make safer decisions under stress.
  • Know the Red Flags: Confidence in identifying danger signs helps parents seek help early and avoid both delay and overreaction.
